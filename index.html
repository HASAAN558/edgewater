Who We Are

Principals who work every deal alongside our clients.

Edgewater was founded in 2008 with a focus on providing distressed advisory services to lenders. Since its formation, it has grown into a multidisciplinary real estate firm that provides acquisition, distressed advisory and management services. Edgewater's unique focus is in real estate within the micro and middle market segments in secondary and tertiary locations.

Edgewater's representative clients include Federal and State Courts, lenders, investors, and micro and middle market companies. Services include, but are not limited to, receiverships, restructuring, turnarounds, real estate acquisitions, property management and property disposition.

We are unique in that our principals actively participate in every project we undertake. Our senior advisors provide broad and deep industry experience and relationships that benefit our clients — assuring long-term stability and success is our goal.

What We Do

Two disciplines. One customized approach for every client.

Solutions are designed and reported around each client's specific mandate — with our advisors providing the industry-specific financial and operating expertise to execute.

01 / Advisory & Management

Advisory & Management

We perform receivership and foreclosed-asset management functions for Federal and State Courts, lenders and investors — actively managing underlying assets to improve yield and value, with detailed accounting for full transparency and accountability.

  • Expert witness testimony
  • Loan workouts
  • Business viability
  • Portfolio evaluation
  • Exit strategies
  • Management

Capital
